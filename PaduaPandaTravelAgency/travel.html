Frequently Asked Questions!

Do the Cayman Islands have their own currency?
Yes, but they also use U.S. currency interchangeably.
What are the vaccination requirements?
Vaccinations are not mandatory for visiting the Islands.
Where is there Wi-Fi or Internet?
Most accommodations provide Wi-Fi or Internet.
Do I need a passport?
Yes.
How do I get to the Sister Islands?
Cayman Airways Express has multiple flights between the three islands.
